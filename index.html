About the Little Pizza Place

The LLP is a restaurant dedicated to providing the best quality pizza, while maintaining the low
price and taking into account the importance of the cleanliness of the restaurant in accordance
with health laws. Established in 1983 with a love of satisfying customers and meeting their
requests, whatever they may be.

Pizza fun facts

When was the first pizza ever made?

    The modern Pizza as we know it lately was born in the year 1989.

Who made the first pizza ever?

    The first modern pizza was made by the chef and pizzamaker "Raffaele Esposito".

What type was the first pizza every made?

    The first modern pizza ever made was a “Pizza Margherita” for the Queen consort of Italy, "Margherita of Savoy".

What does pizza literally mean?

    The word in Italian is picea, and literally translated it means “blackening of crust by fire”.
